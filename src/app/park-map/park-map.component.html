About

Huntley Meadows Park is a beautiful, local wildlife sanctuary. Over 200,000 visitors frequent its walking-paths per year. The Park is a biodiverse forest and wetland home to over 30 species of mammals, 50 species of reptiles, amphibians and dragonflies, 200 species of birds and 600 species of plants.
